This tingly solution increases the elasticity and contraction of your leg muscles. For 1 minute after drinking this elixir, you can [High Jump](/Actions.aspx?ID=36) or [Long Jump](/Actions.aspx?ID=37) as a single action instead of 2 actions. If you do, you don’t perform the initial [Stride](/Actions.aspx?ID=88) (nor do you fail if you don’t Stride 10 feet).\n\n
